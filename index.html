About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at the University of Nebraska- Lincoln and a member of the AAC Translation (AACT) Laboratory.  My main research interests include biomedical signal processing, electroencephalogram (EEG) signal analysis, such as brain-computer interfaces (BCI), neurophysiological disease/disorder modeling and prediction (specifically for epilepsy and TBI).

My research interests

  • EEG
  • Signal Processing
  • Machine Learning
  • Bioinformatics
  • Data mining
  • RNA-seq
